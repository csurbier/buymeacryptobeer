F.A.Q for content creators

How can i be sure this website is not a scam and i could trust it ?

This project is opensource and you can read the code on GitHub

The smart contract can be verified Here

I don't have a crypto wallet

To receive donations you need to create a wallet.

The easiests way to do it is to create a Metamask wallet

Then to configure Polygon network

Is it free to create an account ?

Yes ! Creating an account is free.

Because of blockchain technology you still have to pay some minor transactions fees to the network (usually a few cents).

We are using the Polygon network which is an Ethereum layer 2 with low cost transaction fees.

So to create your account you will require to have a least one MATIC in your Metamask wallet to be able to pay the transaction fees.

How to share my donation page with my audience ?

All you have to do is to provide your account url that has been generated for you.

Something like: https://www.buymeacryptobeer.com/yourChoosenName

You can add it on your website as a button, a link, ...

How to retrieve my donations ?

Once your account is created, all you need to do is to connect your wallet and go to your dashboard.

You will see the amount of crypto received and you will have a button to withdraw it (for free).

What is a MATIC and how do i convert it in money such as dollar ?

MATIC is the crypto currency for Polygon network.

You can trade MATIC with real money using exchange platform such as Kraken or Coinbase for the most famous ones.
It's totaly free (minus exchange platform fees).

You must be aware that MATIC coin value is varying in time .
